Our Journey Story

How We Started Our
Journey

We started our journey in the second wave of Covid-19. During the period of the second wave of covid-19, we helped thousands of patients in Aurangabad for getting Ventilator beds, Oxygen cylinders, Medicine and many more. That is how came to know many problems in the healthcare sector in tier 2 cities.

Our Thousands Of
Thoughts

The healthcare ecosystem is highly unorganised and data of fees structure, facilities available and in-clinic appointment booking for nearby hospitals is unavailable in tier 2 cities. So we decided to create a health care ecosystem more affordable, standardized and readily available to everyone in Bharat. This idea turned into a company.
